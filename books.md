# Bible Books

The Bible is a library of many books written over many centuries.

Not all books made it into the modern Bible canon as we know it today.

## Missing books or scriptures

The following passages quote sources that do not exist in our modern bibles:

* Matthew 26:24 / Mark 14:21
* Matthew 27:3-10 (A copy of the prophecy in Zechariah 11:4-17 survived, but the original prophecy from Jeremiah is lost)
* Luke 18:31-33
* Luke 24:46-49
* John 5:38
* Ephesians 5:14
* 2 Corinthians 6:16-18 (Might potentially paraphrase Leviticus 26:12 and Isaiah 52:11, but it is not an exact quote)
