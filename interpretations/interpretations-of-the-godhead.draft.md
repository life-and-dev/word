If you believe Jesus and/or the Holy Spirit is not God, you are considered a "heretic" that [blaspheme](/bible/concepts/blasphemy) against God according to [the council of Nicaea](/kingdom/church/history#the-first-nicaea-creed).

However, if you consider the opposite and belief Jesus and/or the Holy Spirit is God and you are wrong, then you commit [idolatry](/bible/concepts/idolatry) because you worship someone or something other than God Almighty.

Both blasphemy and idolatry are considered very serious [sin](/bible/concepts/sin) against God. So we need to carefully study the evidence to worship God correctly.

# How many members does God have?

* Trinitarians: 3, namely the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit
* Unitarians: 1
